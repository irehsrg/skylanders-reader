    Scan & edit your figures

    Portals connect over USB, which browsers can’t read directly — so scanning runs through a small free desktop app, the Portal Station. Browsing and your collection work right here without it.

    Download Portal Station (Windows)
    1. Unzip it, then plug in your Portal of Power.
    2. Double-click Start Portal.bat.
    3. It opens http://localhost:8777 — scan from there.

    Windows may warn “Windows protected your PC” (the app isn’t code-signed) — click More info → Run anyway. It’s open source.
