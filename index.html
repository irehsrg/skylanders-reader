          How it works
          

            Browse anywhere: sign in and your collection, wishlist and game-completion sync
            across devices — no portal needed.
          

          

            Scan & edit: run the free Portal Station app, which talks to
            your USB Portal of Power, then open http://localhost:8777. Drop figures on the portal
            to auto-identify them; for figures you own you can edit gold, XP, hero points, nickname, or reset —
            always backed up and verified before writing.
